Why Run For Farmers...?

Run For Farmers

India is the prime agricultural land. Food and water are basic needs of human being’s event though it’s all living thing’s basic needs. Those basic needs completed by farmes. In farming men’s role is main, woman’s role is major than men. Farmer is food provider of all and guardian of animals. Farmer is protector of nature and environment. Small farmers, marginals, aboriginal farmers and agricultural laborers are stuffing from same issues.

Today, the entire country’s farmers are in big trouble, mainly Maharatrian farmers are facing death torcher. Farmers are exhausted due to bankruptcy, ignorance, inorganization, infertility etc.In the name of farmers many peoples doing polytics. Some peoples are halping and guiding them as well, many peoples are strugling for farmers good life and give the better life to farmer. “Run For Farmer” marathon is one of the small try, On 1st July Agriculture Day to Save the Farmer.

Run for Farmer and Save the Farmer this Marathon event is in Karad orgnized by Satara District Amateur Athletics Associations  with support of Karad Tehsil Amateur Athletics Associations.
